## Security Surface
Read this before granting access to anyone.

| Item | Risk | Who must NOT have access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Service account with Domain-Wide Delegation] | **HIGH** (impersonates any Workspace user) | `secretmanager.secretAccessor` on its key; `iam.serviceAccountTokenCreator` on it |
| Cloud Build auto-deploy trigger | HIGH (push-to-main ships code) | Direct push to `main`; modify `cloudbuild.yaml` without review |
| [Hot secrets] | MEDIUM | `secretmanager.secretAccessor` unless scoped |
| Firestore write | MEDIUM | `datastore.user` / `datastore.owner` unless required |
